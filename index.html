About Me

For now, I spend time working for Virginia Tech Relay for Life, writing about health and wellness, and keeping up with my Netflix shows.

Meet Kale Yeah!

We are a group of English majors at Virginia Tech, in a Writing and Digital Media class. We work alongside one another to combine our skills to produce projects. We center our content around healthy lifestyles and tips to live a better life.

Dan's Bandana Project at Virginia Tech

Dan's Bandana Project is a program that seeks to bring awareness to mental health and to close the stigmatism surrounding mental health issues. Participants of the Dan's Bandana Project and together by wearing a white bandana on their backpack to signal their support of the project.
