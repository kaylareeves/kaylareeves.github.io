About Me

I am a undergradute student at Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, studying Prelaw: Literature and Language with a minor in Political Science. My next ambition is to attend law school and pursue a J.D. in Health Policy Law. For now, I spend time working for Virginia Tech Relay for Life, writing about health and wellness, and keeping up with my Netflix shows.

Meet Kale Yeah!

We are a group of English majors at Virginia Tech, in a Writing and Digital Media class. We work alongside one another to combine our skills to produce projects. We center our content around healthy lifestyles and tips to live a better life.
